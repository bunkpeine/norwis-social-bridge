Norwis Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Norwis handles data in connection with social media post preparation and manual publishing workflows.

1. Data processed

Norwis may process social media post text, preview content, manually entered platform-related information, locally stored configuration data, and environment variables containing access credentials or API-related values.

2. Local processing

Norwis is designed to process data locally on the user's device wherever possible.

3. Third-party platforms

If connected, Norwis may interact with third-party services such as TikTok or Instagram for content preparation or publishing.

4. No unnecessary sharing

Norwis does not intentionally share user data with unrelated third parties beyond the connected platform workflows initiated by the user.

5. User control

All publishing actions are intended to remain under user control and require manual review and approval.

6. Security

Users are responsible for keeping local systems, credentials, and environment variables secure.

7. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact the app owner directly.
